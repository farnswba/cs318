About BRAYJAY LLC

Based out of Janesville, WI, BrayJay specializes in creating dance parties that will create long lasting memories for you and your guests. We happily work with you one-on-one to create an amazing experience for your event and fulfill all of your desires. Playing the music that you and your guests are interested in and mixing it together to maintain a natural flow throughout the event is something we specialize in. We'll bring the music and lighting, you bring the guests.

Meet the Crew
